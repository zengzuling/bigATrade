# WeChat Daily Review Template

## Title Options

### Conservative

`MM-DD Recommendation Recap: who was strongest and what to watch tomorrow?`

### More viral

`These 5 stocks were reviewed today: who stayed strong and who needs caution?`

### Default recommendation

`5 recommendation stocks in a weak market: who was strong today and what matters tomorrow?`

## Poster Copy Skeleton

Main title:

`Who was strongest among today's 5 recommendation stocks?`

Subtitle:

`YYYY-MM-DD recommendation stock recap`

Market sentence:

`The broader market was under pressure, so true relative strength stood out clearly.`

Short stock labels:

- `Da You Neng Yuan: strongest, active accumulation`
- `Feng Huang Hang Yun: active but faded from highs`
- `Shi Dai Xin Cai: repair move with support`
- `Jin Xin Nuo: high-volatility divergence`
- `Yuan Dong Gu Fen: huge turnover, obvious divergence`

Closing line:

`Today's strongest name was XX; the rest were mostly repair or divergence trades.`

## Article Skeleton

Paragraph 1:

`The market was weak today, but the tracked recommendation stocks already showed clear separation in strength.`

Paragraph 2:

`Use the strongest stock paragraph here. Add one core number and one conclusion.`

Paragraph 3:

`Use the middle-tier names here. Explain support and divergence in one short paragraph.`

Paragraph 4:

`Use the weaker names here. Explain volatility, risk, or lack of consistency.`

Closing paragraph:

`In a weak market, the key is not which stock looks busiest, but which one still shows initiative under pressure.`

## Tomorrow Plan Skeleton

Paragraph 1:

`For the strongest name, focus on whether strength can continue tomorrow.`

Paragraph 2:

`For the middle-tier names, focus on whether repair continues and divergence shrinks.`

Paragraph 3:

`For the weaker names, focus first on whether they can stabilize before discussing rebound potential.`

Closing line:

`Tomorrow's focus is not broad action, but tracking strength first and separating strong from weak.`
